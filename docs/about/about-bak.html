About us

A research engine designed by students for students

Instant information

Harvix provides Instant Information which allows you to receive an overview of the information you're searching for. Harvix supplies a well formed article, facts about the query, data that is relevant to your search and much more. Harvix's Instant Information is so effective you will often find what you're looking for just from the the Instant Information section.

Facts

Harvix has a complex facts algorithm that pulls the most reliable and trustworthy facts and displays them in order of general to specific on any given query. Facts are pulled only from human curated web sites that Harvix deems reliable and accurate.

Categorization

Results on Harvix are seperated into digestable catagories which allows you to find exactly what you are looking for faster. For example the query "Steve Jobs" equals Instant Information, Facts, Twitter, Images, Social, Apple, Investing, Money, Tech, and Latest.

Notes

With Harvix's unique notes feature you can take notes directly from your search page. Now to take notes you don't have to switch from your notes page, and back to your search page every time you want to write a note. In addition, your notes are saved in the cloud, and are accessible from any device.

Spam-free

Harvix only includes sites whose primary purpose is information, not monetization in our web results. Sites created solely to sell you products or steal your money are not included in our web results.

Our mission is to let students discover and share academic content
in simple and meaningful ways.
