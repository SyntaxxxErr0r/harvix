Artist Statement

Photographs are captured moments for various perspectives. They allow the viewer to experience an event from the perspective of the photographer. When I take pictures I like to remember that I am capturing a moment that I can look back on and remeber the event. Pictures enable people to travel distances of time and view the past. My photos allow me to step peer into a world that I cannot physically recreate.

Photography allows us to capture a moment in time
